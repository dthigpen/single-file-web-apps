# Encrypted TOTP Vault

The TOTP Vault is a browser-native two-factor authentication tool. It operates completely offline to generate standard 6-digit security codes from your saved account keys.

---

## Quick Start

Simply open up the app and enter your master password to view your 2FA codes.

## Data Management

Because this application relies entirely on browser storage, your active database state can occasionally be purged by your phone operating system during regular storage cleanups. 

To prevent account loss, establish a routine to save external backups to your physical storage card.

### Backup Steps
1. Navigate to the Manage Vault section within the user interface.
2. Select the export option to compile your database into an encrypted text block.
3. Save the resulting file onto your physical storage drive or device document directory.
4. To restore your data on an alternative device or after a browser cleanup, select the import option and locate your saved file.
